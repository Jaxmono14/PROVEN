## Executive Summary

Proven. competes in a crowded supplement market with two genuine differentiators:
**format** (performance gummies + powders vs. powder-only incumbents) and
**taste** (science-backed formulas that actually taste good). The ad strategy
leverages these to attack from angles competitors aren't owning.

**Primary goal:** Drive first-time purchases with a target ROAS of 3.5–4.5×.  
**Secondary goal:** Build retargeting pools and email/SMS lists for LTV growth.

## Platform Strategy

### Primary: Meta Ads (60% of budget)
**Why:** Best DTC e-commerce platform for visual supplement brands. Advantage+
Sales Campaigns deliver 22% higher ROAS than manual campaigns. Retargeting pool
grows quickly from catalog engagement.

**Approach:** Advantage+ Sales Campaigns (ASC) as primary prospecting engine.
Manual CBO for interest targeting tests. Strong retargeting via custom audiences.

### Secondary: Google Ads (27% of budget)
**Why:** Captures high-intent searchers ("pre workout gummies", "clean supplements",
"best performance supplements"). Brand protection is essential as brand grows.
Performance Max for shopping discovery.

**Approach:** Brand Search (always-on) + Non-brand Search (high-intent keywords)
+ Performance Max (shopping). No YouTube until Month 5+ (needs creative investment).

### Testing: TikTok Ads (13% of budget)
**Why:** CPMs 40–60% cheaper than Meta. Sports supplement content performs
well organically — Spark Ads amplify existing UGC. TikTok Shop creates
frictionless purchase path. Platform skews 18–34, which is core audience.

**Approach:** Smart+ Catalog campaigns + Spark Ads on top UGC. Creative is the
product on TikTok — needs native-feel content, not polished brand ads.

## Messaging Strategy

### Core Message Architecture

| Funnel Stage | Message | Copy Framework | Audience Temperature |
|--------------|---------|----------------|----------------------|
| Awareness | "Your supplements just got an upgrade" | BAB (Before/After/Bridge) | Cold |
| Consideration | "Science-backed. Clean. Actually delicious." | PAS (Problem/Agitate/Solution) | Warm |
| Conversion | "Clean ingredients. Real results. No excuses." | FAB (Features/Advantages/Benefits) | Hot |
| Retention | "You trained hard. Proven. helped." | Star-Story-Solution | Existing customers |

### 5 Creative Pillars

1. **The Gummy Upgrade** — "Why are you still choking down chalky powder?" Attack
   the inconvenience and taste of traditional powders. Position gummies as the
   smarter format for the modern athlete.

2. **Ingredient Transparency** — Show the label. Explain what's in it and why.
   Science-forward but accessible. "You know what you're putting in your body."

3. **Real Results, Real People** — UGC from actual gym-goers. Not models.
   Authentic sweaty gym videos with genuine reactions to taste. Social proof
   drives purchase intent more than polished brand ads.

4. **The Clean Performance Trade-off Doesn't Exist** — "Why do supplements have
   to taste like punishment?" Address the belief that effective = unpleasant.
   Proven. breaks that assumption.

5. **Recovery as Self-Respect** — Position recovery supplements as an act of
   respecting your body and your hard work. Emotional angle for mid-funnel.
