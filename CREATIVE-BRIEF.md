### Pillar 3: Real Results, Real People (UGC)
*Framework: Star-Story-Solution*  
*Funnel: Mid/Bottom — Consideration & Conversion*

- **Star:** Real gym-goer, runner, amateur athlete (not a model)
- **Story:** Was skeptical. Tried it. Couldn't believe how good it tasted.
- **Solution:** Still using it 3 months later. PRs went up.

**UGC production notes:**
- Film in actual gyms, not studios
- Genuine reactions to tasting > scripted testimonials
- Handle held, slightly shaky = authentic
- No voiceover. On-screen text captions only.
- Athlete should look sweaty, mid-workout or post-workout

## Platform-Specific Creative Specs

### Meta
| Placement | Ratio | Max File Size | Video Length |
|-----------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| Feed (image) | 1:1 or 4:5 | 30 MB | — |
| Stories / Reels | 9:16 | 4 GB | 15s (ideal), max 60s |
| Carousel | 1:1 | 30 MB per card | — |

- Text overlay: ≤20% of image area
- Safe zone: Keep text 14% from top and bottom on Stories/Reels
- Always include captions on video (80%+ watch without sound)

### Google RSA
| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Headlines | 30 chars × 15 (use all 15) |
| Descriptions | 90 chars × 4 (use all 4) |
| Sitelinks | 25-char link text, 35-char descriptions |

- Pin headline 1 to brand name for brand campaigns
- Mix benefit headlines, feature headlines, and CTA headlines

### TikTok
| Element | Spec |
|---------|------|
| Ratio | 9:16 only |
| Resolution | 1080×1920 minimum |
| Duration | 9–15s (ideal); max 60s |
| Caption | ≤100 chars, include CTA |

- Hook must appear in first 2 seconds
- Never use polished brand-ad aesthetic (gets skipped)
- Captions essential — 80%+ watch on mute

## Copy Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Use "you/your" — directly address the athlete
- Include a specific claim with backing ("clinically-dosed creatine", "0g artificial sweeteners")
- Short sentences. Three words work. 
- One CTA per ad — never stack two asks

**DON'T:**
- "Clinically proven to cure / treat / fix" — supplement compliance issue
- "Best supplement in the world" — unsubstantiated superlative
- Before/after body imagery — Meta policy violation
- Medical claims that imply disease treatment
- Fake urgency ("ONLY 3 LEFT!" when that's not true)

## Creative Fatigue Management

Meta creative lifespan has compressed to **3–4 weeks** for top-of-funnel.
Build a rotating library, not a one-time batch.

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 7-day frequency >3.0 (prospecting) | Pause creative, swap |
| CTR declined >20% over 14 days | Retire creative |
| Creative Similarity Score >60% in ASC | Diversify angles urgently |
| Cost per purchase up >15% week-over-week | Refresh creative first, then check targeting |
