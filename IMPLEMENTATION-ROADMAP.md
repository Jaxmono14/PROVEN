## Phase 2: Launch (Weeks 3–4)

**Order of launch:** Meta first → Google 3 days later → TikTok Week 4

### Week 3: Meta Launch
- [ ] Launch ASC (Advantage+ Sales Campaign) with 5 diverse creatives
  - Start at $100/day, conservative bid
  - Monitor daily: check delivery, CPMs, link clicks
- [ ] Launch Retargeting campaign (even with small pools — builds data)
  - Website visitors 30d, cart abandoners 7d
- [ ] Do NOT touch campaigns for first 7 days (learning phase is active)
- [ ] Verify conversions recording in Meta Events Manager

### Week 3 (Days 4–7): Google Launch
- [ ] Launch Brand Search campaign ($10–15/day)
- [ ] Launch Non-brand Search (3 ad groups) at $30–40/day total
- [ ] Launch Performance Max at $30/day
- [ ] Bidding: Maximize Clicks initially (switch to Maximize Conversions at 15+ conv/month)
- [ ] Negative keywords: add brand terms to PMax exclusions, add irrelevant terms

### Week 4: TikTok Launch
- [ ] Launch Smart+ Catalog campaign ($25/day)
- [ ] Launch In-Feed Prospecting ($15/day, fitness interest)
- [ ] Monitor spend delivery and click-through (not conversions yet — learning phase)

### Daily Monitoring (First 2 Weeks)
- CPM spike? Audience too narrow or creative not resonating
- CTR < 1% on Meta? Creative hook is failing — pause and swap
- Zero conversions after $150 spend on a creative? Apply 3× kill rule
- Learning phase reset? Did you change budget >20%? Roll back.

## Phase 3: Optimize (Weeks 5–8)

### Creative Analysis
- [ ] Identify top 2 performing creatives by CPA on Meta ASC
- [ ] Pause bottom 50% of creatives (by CPA or zero spend/conversions)
- [ ] Produce 3–4 new creatives from Batch 2 (UGC compilation, ingredient story)
- [ ] Check Creative Similarity Score — if >60%, diversify angles
- [ ] Monitor frequency: if 7-day frequency >3.0, refresh creative

### Bidding Progression
- [ ] Google: switch to Maximize Conversions if ≥15 conv/month
- [ ] Meta ASC: evaluate bid cap if CPA running too high in learning
- [ ] TikTok: assess if Smart+ is converting; if not, shift budget to Spark Ads

### Audience Expansion
- [ ] Build 1% Lookalike from Purchasers (once ≥100 purchasers in pixel)
- [ ] Build 1% LAL from High-Value Customers (top 25% by LTV)
- [ ] Add Manual CBO Interest campaign with 2 new interest sets

### A/B Testing (Run One Test at a Time)
| Week | Test | Variable |
|------|------|----------|
| 5 | Landing page | Product page vs. dedicated landing page |
| 6 | Creative angle | Gummy Upgrade (BAB) vs. Clean Label (FAB) |
| 7 | Audience | Interest-based vs. Broad ASC |
| 8 | CTA | "Shop Now" vs. "Try Proven." vs. "Get Yours" |

### Kill/Scale Decisions
- Apply 3× Kill Rule: pause any creative/ad set spending 3× target CPA with 0 conversions
- Apply 20% Scale Rule: increase winning ad sets by 20% if CPA < target by >10%

## Reporting Cadence

(multi-touch attribution), platform-native dashboards for creative performance.
