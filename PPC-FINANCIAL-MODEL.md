## 4. LTV:CAC Analysis

### Assumptions
- 3 purchases/year at AOV $55
- Average customer lifespan: 2 years (supplement repurchase typical)
- Gross margin: 60%

```
LTV = AOV × Purchases/Year × Lifespan Years
LTV = $55 × 3 × 2 = $330 gross revenue
LTV (margin-adjusted) = $330 × 0.60 = $198
```

### LTV:CAC by Tier

| Tier | Monthly Spend | New Customers | CAC | LTV | LTV:CAC | Payback |
|------|--------------|--------------|-----|-----|---------|---------|
| Tier 1 ($5K) | $5,000 | ~189 | $26 | $198 | **7.6:1** | ~3.8 mo |
| Tier 2 ($10K) | $10,000 | ~360 | $28 | $198 | **7.1:1** | ~4.1 mo |
| Tier 3 ($20K) | $20,000 | ~695 | $29 | $198 | **6.8:1** | ~4.2 mo |

> **Payback period** = CAC ÷ (monthly gross profit per customer)  
> Monthly gross profit = ($55 × 3/12) × 0.60 = $8.25/month/customer

### LTV:CAC Interpretation

| Ratio | Status | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| <1:1 | Critical | Losing money on every customer |
| 1–2:1 | Warning | Break-even to marginal |
| 3:1 | Target | Healthy (industry minimum) |
| **6–8:1** | **Excellent** | **Proven.'s projected range** |
| >10:1 | Under-investing | Could scale more aggressively |

**Proven.'s projected 6.8–7.6:1 ratio is excellent.** This means for every $1 spent acquiring a customer, they return $6.80–7.60 in gross margin over their lifetime. There is strong justification to invest aggressively in growth.

> **Upgrade path:** At AOV $75 (bundle) and 3 purchases/year, LTV rises to $270 margin-adjusted → LTV:CAC improves to **9.4–10.4:1** at all tiers. Bundle offer ads should be a priority.

## Key Recommendations

1. **Bundle to $75 AOV.** A $55 single-product CPA target is thin. Bundles (pre-workout + recovery pack) push AOV to $75+ and improve LTV:CAC to 9:1+. Lead ads with bundle offers.

2. **Google is underbudgeted at Tier 1.** $1,350/month at $1.15 CPC = ~1,174 clicks. You need more volume to exit Smart Bidding learning phase (15+ conv/month minimum). Push Google to $1,800+ as early as possible.

3. **LTV economics are strong — invest aggressively.** 7:1 LTV:CAC with 4-month payback means the business can afford to acquire customers at up to $66 CPA and still hit 3:1 LTV:CAC. Don't kill campaigns at $35 CPA if LTV is $198.

4. **Install post-purchase survey immediately.** MER tracking requires knowing total revenue. Post-purchase survey fills the attribution gap that platform tracking misses (~30%).

5. **TikTok should be evaluated on assisted conversions, not direct ROAS.** Direct TikTok ROAS of 0.55× looks bad but its influence on Meta and Google conversions via view-through is not captured in platform data. Use MER as the true north star.
